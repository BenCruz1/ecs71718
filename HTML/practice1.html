Martin Luther King Jr.

M. L. K.

Martin Luther King Jr. was an activist, who did his job with pacifism.
His fame began with the start of the bus boycott.
One important moment on his life, still remembered today is his I have a dream speech.
His life was cut short, as he was assassinated, but his dream for equality came true.

5 Achievements

1. He fought for equal rights without using violence

2. He became the youngest recipient of the Nobel Peace Prize

3. He led the Montgomery bus boycott

4. His "I have a Dream" speech is still recognized today

5. He was arrested fighting for equality
